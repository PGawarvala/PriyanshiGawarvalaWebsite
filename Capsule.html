Capsule is planning to be the largest civic hackathon in the world focused on the climate crisis. 4,000 brains solving 6 challenges with 500 projects on the climate crisis.

Scheduled for June 2020 in Austin, Texas and hosted by Experimental Civics, the goal of this event is to bring together technologists from around the world to build technology-based solutions to problems at the intersection of the environment and food, art, energy, health, cities, and education
